01 ── ABOUT THE JAM

What is Geek n Jam?

A month-long pan-India game development jam organized by Geekroom SRMIST. Solo devs, small teams, artists, designers — build something unforgettable from scratch, then show it off at SRMIST.

◈ THEME: INVERTED ◈

Mechanics, narratives, gravity — flip something expected on its head.

Full build period

05 ── JUDGING

Judging Categories

🌀
Best Use of Theme
Awarded to the team that interprets and integrates the theme “Inverted” in the most creative, meaningful, or unexpected way.
🎮
Best Gameplay Design
Focused on how fun, polished, balanced, and engaging the core gameplay loop is. Controls, level flow, and game feel.
Game Feel Mechanics Flow
🎨
Best Art Direction
Focused on overall visual identity and artistic consistency — including UI, environments, color language, and animation style.
📖
Best Narrative
Awarded to the game with the strongest storytelling, worldbuilding, emotional impact, character writing, or narrative structure.
🎵
Best Music & Audio
Recognizes exceptional soundtracks, environmental ambience, sound effects, voice work, and overall soundscapes.
🚀
Best Technical Innovation
Rewards unique technical implementation, procedural systems, advanced shaders, clever rendering, or interesting engine-level work.
Procedural Shaders AI
⚙️
Best Technical Optimization
Demonstrates technical efficiency, performance stability, low resource usage, clever compression, or low-end hardware compatibility.
Low-end Efficiency
🐣
Best Rookie Game
For beginners or first-time game jam participants who show strong creative spark, technical potential, and execution.
👥
Community Voted
Chosen directly by community voting and player feedback after playable game builds are officially released on Itch.
